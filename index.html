About Me

I'm a junior electrical engineer at Texas A&M University with a strong passion for power systems engineering and energy market analysis. With industry experience and a minor in computer science, I bring a unique blend of skills to the table. I enjoy coding, both on the backend and front end, and I'm constantly eager to learn and stay updated with the latest advancements in the field. Through my personal website, I aim to share my insights, experiences, and projects, connecting with like-minded individuals who are passionate about the intersection of electrical engineering, computer science, and energy markets. Let's shape the future of sustainable energy systems together.
